# 🤖 Slack Daily Digest Bot aka TLDRbot

**Get AI-powered summaries of your Slack messages!**

---

## 🚀 Two Steps to Get Started

### 1️⃣ Connect Your Account (1 minute)

Click here: **https://jordan-spleenier-bigotedly.ngrok-free.dev/install**

Click **"Sign in with Slack"** → Click **"Allow"**

> **Block employees:** If you see "not authorized to install", click **"Request approval"**. An admin will approve it within 1 business day, then you can try again.

### 2️⃣ Get Your Summary

In any Slack channel, type:

```
@TLDRbot give me a digest
```

The bot will reply with an AI summary of your last 24 hours! ✨

---

## 📊 What You Get

Your personalized summary includes:

- **🎯 Action Items** — Things you need to do
- **📢 Key Updates** — Important announcements  
- **ℹ️ FYIs** — Good-to-know information

Powered by Claude AI with adaptive thinking.

---

## 🔒 Privacy

- The bot only sees channels **you've** joined
- Uses **your** Slack permissions (sees what you see)
- Summaries posted in threads (only you can see them)
- No message data is stored
