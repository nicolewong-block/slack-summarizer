## Tips for Sharing

**Where to post:**
- Team Slack channels
- Engineering channels
- #tools or #productivity channels
- Direct messages to interested teammates

**When to post:**
- Monday mornings (people looking for productivity tools)
- After a busy week (when people are overwhelmed)
- After admin approval (follow-up announcement)

**Include:**
- Clear call-to-action (the install link)
- Expected time commitment (1-2 minutes)
- Privacy/security info (especially for Block employees)
- Your contact info for questions

**Avoid:**
- Posting in too many channels at once (feels spammy)
- Over-hyping (keep it practical and honest)
- Forgetting to mention the admin approval step for Block employees
